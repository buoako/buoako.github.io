What is Anakbayan?

A national democratic movement created to mobilize the youth to fight for Filipinx human rights. We recognize education, jobs, land, rights, and social services as the immediate and long-term problems of the Filipinx youth. Anakbayan is part of the struggle to end the semi- colonial and semi-feudal society and to win the struggle for national democracy towards socialism.
